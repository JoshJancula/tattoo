My name is Michael Trimboli, also known as Ike. Originally from baltimore MD, I moved to Charlotte at a young age and I've been here ever since. In high school I discovered my love of tattoos and strived to become a tattoo artist since. After many years of pushing myself and learning as much as I could about this industry, I am finally where I am today. I've been tattooing since July 2012.
I specialize in black & grey, color, realism, illustrative realism, neo traditional and traditional. I also specialize in cover ups, scar cover ups as well as mastectomy scar cover ups and I am proud to be involved in The Inked Phoenix Project.
